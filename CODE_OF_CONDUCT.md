# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors and maintainers to keep discussion technical, respectful, and direct.

Examples of expected behavior:

- assume good intent, but challenge ideas with evidence
- give actionable review feedback
- keep disagreement focused on code, product decisions, and facts
- respect contributor time by being specific about bugs, regressions, and tradeoffs

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- personal attacks, insults, or harassment
- hostile or intentionally demeaning language
- repeated off-topic disruption
- publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This applies to project spaces such as issues, pull requests, code review, discussions, and other official communication channels for `software-factory`.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

For serious concerns, contact the maintainers through the security contact listed in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
